ABOUT VENTTOOLS

Built for real ductwork installation

VentTools is an independent collection of HVAC calculators and installation-reference tools designed around the measurements and decisions that arise on site.

What VentTools does

VentTools converts published manufacturer information into clearer calculation steps, opening dimensions and practical installation notes. It also provides general ductwork calculators such as offset and square-to-round tools.

Independent by design

VentTools is not owned, approved or endorsed by any manufacturer shown on the website. Manufacturer names and product references are used only to help users select and verify the relevant published installation method.
