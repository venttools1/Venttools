Cookie Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

Current website storage

This release stores a small preference on your device so VentTools can remember your cookie choice. The website may also use browser storage needed for normal app behaviour and service-worker caching so pages and assets load reliably.

Essential technologies

Essential storage supports security, network delivery, saved consent choices and core site operation. It cannot be switched off through the preference panel because the website may not function correctly without it.

Analytics

Analytics is optional. Google Analytics is connected to the consent control and only loads after you choose Accept analytics. Microsoft Clarity is not included in this release.

Advertising cookies

No advertising cookies are included in this release. Before Google AdSense or another advertising service is enabled, VentTools will need the relevant advertising disclosures and an appropriate consent-management solution for visitors in locations where consent is required.

Change your choice

You can review or change the optional analytics preference at any time.
